How this estimate works

01
Customs Duty
Estimated using average duty rates per product category. EU calculates duty on CIF value (product + shipping). EU de minimis: €150. US de minimis: $800.
02
VAT / Tax
EU VAT applied on (product + shipping + duty). Rates: DE 19%, FR 20%, NL 21%. For US-bound: no federal VAT — state sales tax applies separately.
03
Shipping
Estimated by total shipment weight using average economy/standard carrier rates. Actual rates depend on carrier, service level, dimensions, and volume.
04
FX Buffer
A 2% buffer for currency conversion between USD and EUR. Actual FX fluctuation depends on timing, payment provider, and conversion method.
Estimate Only — Not Financial Advice — This calculator provides directional estimates based on average duty rates by product category, standard VAT rates, and estimated shipping costs. Actual landed costs may vary based on specific HS code classification, product composition, carrier rates, exchange rate fluctuations, applicable trade agreements, and regulatory requirements. For precise, product-level calculations, globecart.one's platform will use real-time data and automated HS classification. All amounts shown in USD.
