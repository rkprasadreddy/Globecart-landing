The Problem

Cross-border breaks because trust breaks — on both sides.

  • 💸
    Buyers get blindsided at delivery

    Surprise duties, VAT, and customs holds at delivery. Refused deliveries cost sellers both-way shipping and often the product itself.

  • 📉
    Margins bleed across invisible layers

    FX, VAT timing, returns, fee stacking, fulfillment structure — margin doesn't vanish in one fee. It erodes everywhere. Reconciliation across 4-6 tools is the real pain.

  • 📦
    Returns are broken or abandoned entirely

    $30 product, $45 return shipping. Sellers refund without returns because reverse logistics is economically irrational. Returns are manually optimized, localized, or avoided.

  • 🔒
    "Localize or don't expand"

    When outcomes are unpredictable, sellers choose the safest strategy: avoid the market entirely. Most US sellers aren't on EU yet. Uncertainty — not cost — is what stops them.
